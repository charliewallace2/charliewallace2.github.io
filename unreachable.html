Unreachable Place Project

August 2025 - December 2025


Description:

This is an experience made in Unreal of an “unreachable place”; between the keys of a keyboard, some of which with their own small environments to explore. There are three environments the player can explore: a magical fantasy cave, the throne room of a castle, and an underwater ravine. This project is more of a “walking simulator”, so there is no goal or objective other than experiencing the three environments. For this project, I learned about Unreal’s procedural generation, terrain, and sound design tools. I encountered many obstacles in the original scope of my project that I had to re-evaluate and redirect for, giving me the opportunity to iterate my idea and design a couple times over until I ended up with something I was proud of.
